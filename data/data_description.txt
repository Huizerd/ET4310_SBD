Relevant columns: AllNames (X), DATE (B)

Column descriptions

AllNames:
- Name1, Offset1; Name2, Offset2; ...
- List of all proper names (people, organizations, events, named dates) with the character offset of their mentioning in the text
- Multiple mentions are separate
- Designed to be maximally inclusive, err on the side of inclusion

DATE:
- YYYYMMDDHHSS
- Date of publication (not date/time of event described)
- '0' indicates unknown/not relevant
